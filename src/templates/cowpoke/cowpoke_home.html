Cowpoke

An App to help manage the Artificial Insemination (AI) of dairy cows. (Apologies to anyone expecting a dubious bovine application of so-called Artificial Intelligence.)

While this is a Demo website exploring the use of HTMX, this App solves a real-world business case. It is developed in consultation with an AI business.

NOTE: Data entered will not persist. After 15 minutes of inactivity, the web server will shut down, deleting the database.

Chosen data will display here.

Written with HTMX served by FastAPI.
